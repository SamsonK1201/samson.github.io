Controls

Move with WASD or Arrow Keys. Click to plant the current crop you have purchased. Once you have planted the crop, it will grow and automatically harvest.

Information

Your money and exp will be displayed in the top left, along with how much you need for the next upgrade. Once you have gained enough money and exp for the next upgrade, you can go to the shop to purchase a new crop. Higher tier crops will have a higher yield and exp gain. When you reach the goal of $40,000 there will be an option to either keep farming or to reset your data and start again.
